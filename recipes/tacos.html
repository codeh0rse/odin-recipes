Tacos Recipe

This taco packs a powerful burst of flavor that will leave no one indifferent. As it takes only 15 minutes to make, everybody will be happy and satiated in no time.

The taco has been around for decades and everybody makes it their own by adding the ingredients they like. This recipe however is the standard variant that has worked well in many households over the world.

  • 1 Cucumber
  • 500g Ground beef
  • 1 can of canned corn
  • Taco spices
  • 2dl Sourcream
  • 1 onion
  • 1 Iceberg Lettuce
  • 12 Taco shells
  • 1 1/2 dl Water
  • A bit of Olive oil
  • Grated Cheese

Steps

  1. Turn on the oven according to the instructions given on the package of the taco shells.
  2. Chop the tomatoes and the cucumber. Peel the onion and chop it as well. Open the corn and strain it through the container. Slice the salad.
  3. Fry the ground beef in the olive oil, and add the taco spices. Pour in the water and let it cook for a few minutes.
  4. Put the taco shells on a tray and heat them in the middle of the oven according to the instrutions on the package.
  5. Add the ground beef to the taco shells, add the cheese and the vegetables. Top it off with the sourcream.
