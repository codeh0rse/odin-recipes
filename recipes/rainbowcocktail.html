Rainbow Cocktail

This cocktail will take you to the rainbow

It's a simple but fun cocktail that has the colors of the rainbow. While it's easy to make, it's sure to impress everyone with it's vivid colors and tropical taste

  • 1/8 parts Vodka
  • 2/8 parts pineapple juice
  • 2/8 Lemon Soda
  • 2/8 parts Blueberry juice
  • 1/8 parts Green Apple juice

Steps

  1. Measure up the vodka and make sure you fill about 1/8 of the glass.
  2. Pour in the lemon soda
  3. Pour in the blueberry juice, make sure not to shake the drink. You don't wanna disturb the layers.
  4. Pour in the pineapple juice and the green apple juice
  5. Slice a lemon and attach it to the rim of the glass
